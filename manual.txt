Press and hold the power button to turn the device on or off.
Press the power button to turn on or lock the screen.
Tap the recents button to open the list of recent apps.
Tap and hold the recents button to access additional options for the current
screen.
Press the home button to turn on the screen while the screen is locked.
Press the home button to return to the Home screen.
Press the home button twice to launch S Voice.
Press and hold the home button to launch Google.
Tap the back button to return to the previous screen.
Press the volume button to adjust the device volume.
